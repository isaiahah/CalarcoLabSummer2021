Gene annotation files from recent literature were stored in this folder, but
are too large to upload.

Data downloaded from:
    - Gerstein et al, 2014
    - Lu et al, 2011
    - Wormbase's WormMine, by searching for C. elegans ncRNA and exporting as .tsv
    - Ensembl's WBcel235.104 assembly, downloaded from NCBI
